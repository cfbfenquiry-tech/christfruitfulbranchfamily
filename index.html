About Us

Christ Fruitful Branch Family (CFBF) is a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ. Founded in 2005, we focus on spiritual growth, community service, and family values.

Our mission is to bear fruit in every season through worship, teaching, and outreach programs.
