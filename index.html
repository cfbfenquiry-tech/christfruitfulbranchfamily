About Us

Christ Fruitful Branch Family (CFBF) is a new vibrant community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ. We are a growing family of believers focused on spiritual growth, community service, and family values.

Our mission is to bear fruit in every season through worship, teaching, and outreach programs. Join us as we build a fruitful branch for God's kingdom!

❯

Our Core Values: Spirit, Light & Truth

Spirit

We are led by the Holy Spirit in everything we do. We pursue His presence, power, and gifts, believing that true transformation and fruitfulness come only through a life surrendered to and empowered by the Spirit of God.

Light

We are called to be bearers of God's light in a dark world. Through authentic love, compassionate service, and bold witness, we shine Christ's hope, joy, and righteousness into our community and beyond.

Truth

We stand firmly on the unchanging Truth of God's Word. The Bible is our foundation for faith, teaching, and living. We seek to know, obey, and proclaim the Truth that sets people free and brings lasting fruit.

Our Services & Programs

Sunday Worship

Step into a powerful encounter with the living God! Our Sunday worship is a Spirit-filled celebration where hearts are ignited, lives are transformed, and faith is strengthened. Expect anointed praise, deep worship, and practical, Truth-centered teaching that equips you to live fruitfully in every area of life. Come hungry for more of Jesus—He is waiting to meet you here. Every Sunday at 10:00 AM.

Bible Study

Journey deeper into the Truth that sets you free! Our Bible Study is a dynamic, Spirit-led gathering where we unpack God's Word together—verse by verse, revelation by revelation. You'll grow in understanding, sharpen your faith, and experience the Light of Scripture illuminating every part of your life. Perfect for those who desire to know God more intimately. Launching soon!

Children's Ministry (Little Lights)

We believe the youngest hearts can shine the brightest for Jesus! Little Lights is a joyful, safe, and Spirit-filled environment where children discover God's love, learn biblical Truth through fun stories, songs, crafts, and games, and grow in faith. Our dedicated team nurtures each child to walk in the Light from an early age, planting seeds that will bear eternal fruit. Launching soon!

Youth Ministry (Ignite Youth)

This generation is called to burn brightly for Christ! Ignite Youth is a vibrant, Spirit-empowered community where young people encounter God powerfully, stand boldly on His Truth, and shine as lights in their schools, homes, and streets. Through dynamic worship, relevant teaching, real talk, and authentic relationships, we raise world-changers who live fully surrendered to the Holy Spirit. Launching soon!
